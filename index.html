This calculator is used to estimate the potential height of children upon reaching adulthood, based on their gender and the height of both parents.
The height is estimated using the following methods:
For boys: Add 13 cm to the mother's height, then add the father's height, and divide the total by 2.
For girls: Subtract 13 cm from the father's height, then add the mother's height, and divide the total by 2.
